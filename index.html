This is an experiment in economics of decision-making. A research foundation has provided funds for conducting this research. Your earning will depend on your decisions. The instructions are simple, and if you follow them carefully and make good decisions, you may earn a considerable amount of money.

During the experiment your payoff will be denominated in experimental points that will be converted into dollars at the end of the experiment at the follow rate:

10 Points = 1 Dollar.

You will start with 50 points.

Your Experimental Task


In this experiment, you will trade with a robot seller.
There are two periods. You can buy one item in each period from a robot seller.

The value of the item for you in each period might be different.
The robot seller doesn't know your true value of the item.

The seller will set a secret price based on the possible values for each period.
The secret price in period 1 might be different from the secret price in period 2.

The cost of producing the item for the seller is zero.

Your Experimental Task


To enter the market, you might need to pay the membership fee.
There is no membership fee in the first period.

Only you know your true value when you enter the market.
You have to make a bid in each period if you are in the market.
